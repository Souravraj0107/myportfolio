About Me

I am Advocate Sourav Raj, a graduate of Indore Institute of Law (B.A. LL.B. Hons.) and Vikram University, Ujjain (LL.M.), specializing in Civil and Revenue Law.

From the early days of my legal journey, I have been passionate about justice, effective drafting, and strong advocacy. My career path includes extensive internships with Senior Advocates, District Courts, NGOs, and Legal Services Authorities, which has given me a broad vision in handling complex disputes.

Along with legal practice, I am deeply interested in literature, philosophy, psychology, and public policy, which shape my holistic approach to law and justice.
